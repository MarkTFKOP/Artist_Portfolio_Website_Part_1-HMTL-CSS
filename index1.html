Alan Walker Brief Summary

Alan Olav Walker also known as DJ Walkzz, is a British-born Norwegian DJ and record producer who is currently been claimed as the streaming king of the electronic music circuit. Through innovative and forward-thinking formats such as online label No copyright Sounds, his music was exposed to fans across the globe. One of the tracks was later re-released as the commercial debut Faded, at the end of 2015 which received Diamond certifications in Germany, Poland and Multi Platinum certifications in over 10 countries. The single quickly spread like wildfire and has since earned multi-platinum certifications around the world, and billions of plays across streaming services. He is ready to start his Aviation Tour with many famous songs such as Alone, The Spectre, Sing me to sleep, Darkside to keep the crowd singing and dancing.
